AuditIQ Blueprint V1 Data Contract

quantity
=
Aggregate Document Quantity
(sum of all line quantities)

amount
=
Aggregate Document Amount
(document total)

unitPrice
=
Effective Document Unit Price
(amount ÷ quantity)

AuditIQ performs document-level matching.

AuditIQ does not perform line-item matching.

Line-item intelligence is outside Blueprint V1 and Blueprint V2 scope.
