# Product Evolution Summary

This document summarizes the chronological progression of AuditIQ from initial concept to an Autonomous Audit Operating System.

## Blueprint V1: Deterministic Intelligence
Established the core business rules. Proved that three-way matching, financial exposure calculation, and policy execution could be handled deterministically.

## Blueprint V2: Extraction Reliability
Secured the intake pipeline. Transitioned experimental OCR logic into a robust confidence-weighted extraction framework, guaranteeing reliable data injection.

## PH-001: Production Hardening
Stabilized the repository. Ensured 100% test coverage, type safety, and framework decoupling before scaling.

## Blueprint V3: Predictive Intelligence
Enabled proactive forecasting. Introduced the Predictive Risk Engine and behavioral predictors. The platform learned to identify exceptions before they finalized.

## Blueprint V4: Autonomous Audit OS
Closed the loop. Integrated SAP and Oracle adapters securely. Connected predictive alerts to the policy execution layer via the `AutonomousRiskIntelligence` service. Surfaced business impact continuously through the `Enterprise Control Tower`.

## Conclusion
AuditIQ is certified as a fully operational enterprise-grade platform. The repository is ready to be tagged as `stable-v4.0.0`.
